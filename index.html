About Trinsore Events

A Legacy of Elegance and Flawless Execution

Trinsore Events is a premium Nigerian bridal and event planning brand. We specialize in curating luxury experiences for weddings, milestone events, and corporate celebrations, taking the stress away from clients so they can focus on making beautiful memories.

We pride ourselves on attention to detail, styling brides with custom hand-crafted bridal beads, professionally tied Gele, and exquisite makeup. Our full-scale event coordination and catering services ensure that every aspect of your celebration—from visual splendor to culinary delight—is executed to perfection.
